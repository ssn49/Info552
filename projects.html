Farm Fresh

In the modern world, almost every product that is available can be bought online with the ease of Fingertips. People are gradually trusting these technologies & thereby encouraging many startups for better ideas that improve the human life. As buying online can be done within minutes & at one's fingertips, it can be considered as beneficial to the consumers. Thus, not only it saves time, avoids traffic & crowd, gives better payment options with free shipping to the doorsteps.

The application "Farm Fresh" is an app where you can shop better quality vegetables and fruits from the comfort of the couch. It will be designed to help people of all ages to purchase daily needed fresh fruits & vegetables online from their home or offices. The Company will provide the rates according to the local market with available offers,
