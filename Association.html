User Experience Group

A group which is a vibrant gathering place for professionals in various design-related roles, including Visual Designers who create visually appealing designs, Product Designers shaping user experiences, UX Designers enhancing product usability, UI Designers crafting functional interfaces, and welcoming UX Researchers and Writers. The community fosters collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and engaging discussions among its diverse members, providing a supportive platform for learning and collective contributions to the advancement of design disciplines.

Premium Career Group

A LinkedIn group, accessible to all LinkedIn Premium Career subscribers, serves as a thriving community for job seekers and professionals. The group aims to facilitate the exchange of job-seeking best practices, career management insights, and general professional advice. With over 2 million members worldwide, most of whom are actively seeking employment, the focus is on sharing stories, tips, and guidance to assist as many community members as possible in their career journeys. By fostering a collaborative environment, the group strives to empower individuals with valuable resources and support to navigate the job market and advance their careers effectively
