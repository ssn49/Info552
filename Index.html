Hello, I'm Sushil S Nisale
The urge in trying out new possibilities in life has always been a thrilling experience for me.
After completing graduation  in Hospitality Studies and Catering Services in 2016 ,I started my own Business - Rajmudra Tours  with adequate experience in the field.  I sensed a need of developing an online platform while running the business, for my travel customers and that triggered my interest in UX design.
 
My interest in User Experience and Interaction Design is driven by my desire to build effective interaction between humans and machines, and my love for modern applications helping customers and businesses with information at their fingertips.
 
I always like to  apply human-centered design skills & knowledge through my designs and help customers across industries maximize their business potential by helping and servicing them using my talent and expertise.
 
I hope you’ll enjoy viewing my projects as much as I enjoyed working on them.
